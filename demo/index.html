How long can you actually pay attention?

We benchmark the models. We trace the agents. The one component nobody profiles is you — the human the whole loop waits on. This is a 60-second audit of your sustained attention, scored like a lab instrument. Nothing leaves this page.

Protocol
  1. Digits flash, one at a time, for one minute.
  2. Press space (or tap) for every digit…
  3. …except 3. When you see a 3, do nothing.
  4. It is boring on purpose. Boredom is the test.

Runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no analytics, no mercy.
